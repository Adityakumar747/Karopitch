# Karo Pitch – Demo Landing Page

This project is a **demo landing page for Karo Pitch**, created as part of an internship assignment by **KaroStartup**.

Karo Pitch is an initiative designed to help **early-stage founders from across India pitch their startups directly to investors**. The goal of this landing page is to clearly explain the platform and encourage startups to apply.

---

## 🚀 About the Project

Karo Pitch aims to bridge the gap between **startup founders and investors**, especially for entrepreneurs from **Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities across Bharat** who often lack access to funding opportunities.

This landing page demonstrates how the platform could present its mission, process, and opportunities to founders and investors.

The design focuses on:
- clean and modern UI
- simple navigation
- startup ecosystem vibe
- mobile-friendly layout

---

## 🛠 Technologies Used

This project was built using:

- **HTML5**
- **CSS3**
- **JavaScript**

No frameworks were used. The goal was to create a **lightweight and responsive landing page using core web technologies**.

---

## 📌 Website Sections

The homepage includes the following sections:

### 1️⃣ Hero Section
A strong introduction to the platform with a headline and call-to-action buttons.

### 2️⃣ About Karo Pitch
Explains the purpose of the platform and its mission to support founders across India.

### 3️⃣ How It Works
A simple 4-step process explaining how startups can pitch their ideas to investors.

- Apply with your pitch deck  
- Get shortlisted by KaroStartup  
- Pitch live to investors  
- Raise funding and scale  

### 4️⃣ Who Can Apply
Startup categories that are eligible to participate:

- D2C Brands  
- Consumer Startups  
- MSMEs  
- SaaS Startups  
- Manufacturing Businesses  
- Bharat-focused startups  

### 5️⃣ Investors Section
A placeholder section representing investors who will attend the pitch events.

### 6️⃣ Featured Startups
Example startup cards displaying sample startups and categories.

### 7️⃣ About KaroStartup
Information about KaroStartup as a startup storytelling platform that has shared thousands of founder journeys.

### 8️⃣ Final Call To Action
Encourages founders to apply and participate in Karo Pitch.
